Security

For questions, concerns or issues with your profile, or to report another user or profile, please send an email to info@miskme.com.

Misk wants you to know we take the security of our applications and that of your private data very seriously. While it's impossible to predict any potential cyber threat to our infrastructure, we're working around the clock to ensure our community's data is safe.

Security Practices

Misk utilizes physical, procedural and electronic controls in connection with the protection of our systems and user data from unauthorized access. This includes regularly having our systems and applications reviewed for potential issues by our internal security team and, in the future, outside security firms.

Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

Misk encourages input from the security research community to help make our applications and user data more secure. We invite security researchers to report any possible vulnerabilities found to this email address. We track the address regularly and respond to submissions within 24-48 hours. If you do not receive a response within the timeline, we ask that you resubmit your vulnerability report. For the protection of our users, we ask you to avoid sharing the details you have learned about any possible vulnerabilities with anyone outside Misk until we investigate and confirm that this vulnerability has been addressed properly. To encrypt your report, you can use the PGP key below.

Our bug bounty program is invitation-only. The inclusion criteria involve submitting quality reports and following generally accepted accountable reporting practices. Scientists who meet those conditions are invited to join the program. Please be aware that Misk expressly prohibits Denial of Service (DoS) testing or the use of automated scanning software against any of our applications or infrastructures.
