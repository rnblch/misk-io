For questions, concerns or issues with your profile, or to report another user or profile, please send an email to info@miskme.com

Misk wants you to know that we take the security of our applications and your private data very seriously. While it is impossible to anticipate every possible future cyber threat against our infrastructure, we work around the clock to ensure that our community's data is safe.

Security Practices

Misk employs physical, procedural and electronic controls in connection with the protection of our systems and user data from unauthorized access. This includes regularly having our systems and applications reviewed for potential issues by our internal security team and outside security firms.

Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

Misk encourages input from the security research community to help improve the security of our applications and user data. We invite security researchers to responsibly disclose any potential vulnerabilities uncovered to this email address. We regularly monitor this address and will respond to submissions within 24-48h. If you do not receive a response within the timeframe, we ask that you resubmit your vulnerability report. For the safety of our users, we request that you please avoid sharing the information you have found about any potential vulnerabilities with anyone outside of Misk, until we have investigated and confirmed that such vulnerability has been properly addressed.
